Dee Tree come from Minsk, Belarus.

It started off as jam sessions where we realized how much we had in common, including our views on life and love for nerdy board games. On top of that, we shared the strong urge to dive deep into soul music and through it to express what we had in mind. And we're still exploring, every single day.
